About Free Sonic Collection PC

Free Sonic Collection PC is a freeware/opensource collection of Sonic games for PC created by fans. This project is meant to promote those fans and also advertise Sega's iconic character Sonic. The games included in the collection are free/demo and legal. This collection does not intend on hurting Sega's sales.
